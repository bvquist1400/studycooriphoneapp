About StudyCoor

StudyCoor is a professional pharmaceutical study coordination tool designed for clinical research coordinators, study managers, and pharmaceutical professionals.

Key Features:

  • Medication compliance calculator with multiple dosing frequencies
  • Subject and study management (Pro)
  • Calculation history with step-by-step explanations
  • CSV and PDF export capabilities
  • Privacy-first design with local-only data storage
